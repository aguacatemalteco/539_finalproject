About

Patrick Mullen-Coyoy is a writer, college access advocate, and Capricorn currently living out their Saturn return in Ypsilanti, MI. As program manager for the Michigan College Advising Corps, he helps inform, engage, and inspire students across the state to get to and through college. In their free time, they write poems about the moon blowing up, some of which appear in The Acentos Review, Barrelhouse, BOMB Magazine, HAD, and Underblong Journal. Connect with him on Twitter for more gay writing and amateur astrological advice.
